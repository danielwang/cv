The Process

In the ideation phase, I engaged internal staff to participate in a workshop. Participants included product owners, software developers, account managers, client delivery and pre salse and together we focused on the benefits and concerns of the current system.

I introduced the personas that I had developed in a separate project, and we investigated the specific benefits and costs for each of them via an empathy map exercise.We ran a design studio session to generate ideas that we could develop

Next, we produced lo-fi wireframes and sent the usability survey to end users by using online testing tools to validate our concepts.

We follow lean UX process, quickly implement the new designs into Alpha software for users to test
